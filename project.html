About Us

The name "Velvet Bean" represents our core philosophy: coffee should be a smooth, luxurious experience, yet robust enough to leave a lasting impression. From the moment the raw green beans are selected to the final, precise pour, we take a fearless approach to brewing—embracing the risks required to push boundaries and extract the perfect profile from every roast.

The Atmosphere


Velvet Bean is designed to be your urban escape. We’ve curated a space that feels slightly cinematic—a moody, refined backdrop where you can disconnect from the noise of the city, dive into deep work, or share quiet conversations. Whether you are grabbing a quick morning espresso or settling in for a long afternoon, the ambiance is crafted to elevate your daily ritual.
